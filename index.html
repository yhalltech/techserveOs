Common Questions

Q: How long does the installation process take?
A: Typically, a full installation takes 1-2 hours, while dual boot installations may take 2-3 hours.

Q: Do I need to provide the OS license?
A: For Windows installations, you need to provide a valid license. Linux distributions are generally free.

Q: What if I'm not satisfied with the service?
A: We offer a 30-day warranty on our installations. Contact us for assistance.
